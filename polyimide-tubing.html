Ultra-thin-wall polyimide tubing and coated wire in partnership with Keystone Medical Technologies, Inc.

Polyimide Tubing

  • IDs from .007" to .075"
  • Wall thicknesses from .0005" to .0035"
  • PTFE/polyimide composite material available for lubricious IDs or ODs
  • Thermoplastic top coats available in Pebax, nylon, and Tecoflex for increased bondability

Coated Wire

  • Polyimide coated copper wire — wire diameters .007" to .075"
  • Polyimide or Pebax coated 304SST wire — wire diameters .003" to .075"
