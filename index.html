About Us

Custom Engineered Solutions, Backed by Decades of Expertise

The Alexander Sales Group is a manufacturers representative agency providing custom engineered solutions for the medical device and biomedical OEM. The manufacturers we represent deliver outsourced solutions for minimally invasive devices — and work in concert with your project requirements.

Headquartered in St. Paul and founded in 1999 by Bill Alexander, a lifelong Twin Cities resident with more than two decades of experience selling contract manufacturing and custom components in this territory.

  • Thermoplastic, braided, fluoroplastic, stainless & nitinol tubing
  • Machined processes and component finishing
  • Contract manufacturing for finished device assemblies
  • Electromechanical assembly services
