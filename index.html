About Us

Custom Engineered Solutions, Backed by Decades of Expertise

The Alexander Sales Group is a manufacturers representative agency providing custom engineered solutions for medical device OEMs and contract manufacturers alike. Minnesota's Medical Alley is a major hub for contract manufacturing, and we connect our component manufacturers to both sides of the industry — delivering outsourced solutions that work in concert with your project requirements.

Headquartered in St. Paul and led by Bill Alexander, a lifelong Twin Cities resident with decades of experience selling contract manufacturing and custom components in this territory.

  • Thermoplastic, braided, fluoroplastic, stainless & nitinol tubing
  • Machined processes and component finishing
  • Contract manufacturing for finished device assemblies
  • Electromechanical assembly services
